Additional Information:
Site 72 is a containment and research facility established atop the remnants of a decommissioned site embedded deep within a mountain range similar to the Cheyenne Mountains. The only external access is through an old mining silo, complicating any unauthorized access. Originally constructed in 1989 and decommissioned in 1996 due to design concerns and security leaks, Site 72 was repurposed in 2005. Refurbishments completed in 2007 modernised much of the site's structure, however certain sections still boast the original layout.
